About Us
The website helps users find PGs/hostel rooms and roommates. Suppose a person living in Mumbai has found a pg room but the price is very high and thus would like to share the room with someone else, he will post an ad on our site mentioning that he is looking for a roomate. On the other hand if a person is new to a city and is looking for PGs/rooms, they can use our website to find the ad that was posted earlier. Same way a pg owner can post his ad on our site if he wants to rent his room.
