About Us
Our website helps users find PGs/hostel rooms and roommates. A person new to a city with no connections, the first they will be looking for is a house/room to live in. Our site helps such users find PG rooms of their choice, talk to the owner and negotiate the prices, with all of this being done on our site. On the other hand, a user already living in the city wishes to rent their house/room can post an ad on our site. Our region of interset as of now is aimed at Mumbai and will be expanding our base once the number of users on our site increases.
